Our Work Flow

BNAK operates with a structured workflow aimed at empowering small businesses and entrepreneurs across the country. The association prioritizes member registration, where individuals and groups join to access various benefits and services. Regular monitoring and evaluation ensure continuous improvement of programs and services to meet the evolving needs of its members.

Partnerships

Collaborations

The Biashara Ndogo Association of Kenya (BNAK) offers several partnership opportunities for organizations that align with its mission to empower small businesses in Kenya. These partnerships can include:

  • Collaborative Training and Capacity Building: Partnering to provide skill development programs and workshops for small business owners. Offering training on financial management, marketing, and digital tools for growth.
  • Joint Advocacy and Awareness Campaigns: Partnering for public awareness or consumer education campaigns focused on improving business conditions for SMEs. Working together on policy advocacy to create a better regulatory environment for small businesses in Kenya.
  • Access to Funding and Investment: Collaborating with organizations that provide funding or micro-loans to small businesses. Engaging with investors who want to support sustainable, small-scale enterprises in Kenya.
  • Market Access and Business Networks: Establishing networks to connect small businesses with suppliers, distributors, and potential clients.
  • Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Initiatives: Collaborating on CSR projects that support entrepreneurship and the growth of micro and small businesses in Kenya.
  • Technology and Innovation Support: Partnering with tech companies to introduce innovative solutions such as digital payment systems, business management software, or e-commerce platforms.

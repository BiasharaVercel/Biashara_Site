We represent small-scale traders

The Biashara Ndogo Association of Kenya (BNAK) is a registered national organization representing small-scale traders, micro-enterprises, and informal businesses in Kenya.

Our Mission

To champion the interests of small businesses by providing access to resources, training, market opportunities, and advocating for a favourable business environment.

Advocacy and Representation

Promotes fair and business-friendly policies for small-scale traders.

Capacity Building

Provides training in financial literacy, business management, and technology adoption.

Access to Finance

Partners with financial institutions to provide affordable credit facilities tailored to small businesses.

Our Services

Aligned with our vision, our services are thoughtfully designed to alleviate the complexities of business operations, empowering members to focus on growing their enterprises without undue concern over administrative burdens.

Market Linkages

Creates platforms for small businesses to showcase their products and services.

Regulation and Standards

Works closely with institutions to ensure compliance and affordability.

Technology Integration

Encourages digital solutions for payments, marketing, and inventory management.

Partnerships

BNAK collaborates with government agencies, private sector organizations, NGOs, and development partners to support member growth and sustainability.

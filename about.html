About

Focus Sectors for Biashara Ndogo Association of Kenya (BNAK):

1. Agribusiness and Agro-Processing: BNAK is dedicated to empowering small-scale farmers and agri businesses by promoting modern farming practices, value addition, and access to local and international markets. We support initiatives in horticulture, dairy, poultry, and fisheries to enhance productivity and profitability.

2. Trade and Retail: We champion the interests of small traders in retail shops, wholesale, and open-air markets. BNAK works to address challenges like high taxation, unfair competition, and market access, creating a favorable environment for growth.

3. Manufacturing and Cottage Industries: Supporting artisans, craftsmen, and small-scale manufacturers is at the core of our mission. BNAK focuses on enhancing local production in textiles, leather, furniture, and handicrafts, driving innovation and market access.

4. Digital Economy and E-Commerce: Recognizing the importance of technology, BNAK promotes digital adoption for small businesses. This includes e-commerce platforms, digital payment systems, and online marketing tools to enhance competitiveness.

5. Renewable Energy and Green Technologies: BNAK advocates for small businesses in solar energy, clean cooking solutions, and waste management. We aim to promote sustainable and eco-friendly practices among SMEs.

6. Hospitality and Tourism: Supporting community-based tourism and small hospitality businesses is a key focus. BNAK works to enhance the visibility and sustainability of enterprises in lodging, catering, and tour operations.

7. Creative Economy: BNAK promotes SMEs in the creative industry, including , fashion, film, and art. We provide platforms for these entrepreneurs to access markets and protect their intellectual property.

8. Health and Wellness: We focus on supporting small businesses in pharmaceuticals, fitness, mental health, and organic health products. Our goal is to promote affordable health solutions for entrepreneurs and their communities.

9. Construction and Real Estate: BNAK supports SMEs in construction, hardware supply, and real estate development. We advocate affordable housing and sustainable building practices.

10. Financial Services and Fintech: BNAK focuses on improving access to affordable credit, microfinance, and savings solutions for small businesses while encouraging digital payment adoption and financial literacy.
